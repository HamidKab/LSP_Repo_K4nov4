Coming into college AI was on the raise and as a up and coming engineer that wanted to stand out and reject it. Over the course of my college career I have slowly been able to accept it more and more. At the start of this course I was surprised to learn about the AI policy. I accepted but with the strong head I have I would always toss it to the side at first and attempt to tackle problems on my own. Throughout this semester I have been able to see AI as a tool rather than my adversary and make better use of it. 

In the future I plan to continue using AI to help me with projects and become a better rounded engineer. One specific project I have in mind is a personal project my friend asked me to help him build. It is a video sentiment analyzer that timestamps clips that would be the best for content on social media. In my head I already have the pipeline planned out and I plan to use AI to help me flesh it out.I have come to understand a good engineer is not one that fights changes, but rather one that embraces change with open arms and learns to flourish in the new environment.
